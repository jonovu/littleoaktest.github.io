An exquisite, bespoke event space.

At dusk, lit spectacularly, Top Paddock transforms into an unforgettable backdrop for your city wedding, corporate function or private party. Our services are all inclusive and tailored to your individual needs. Exclusive use of the venue from 6.30pm till 1am ensures the space is yours to enjoy for an unforgettable night.
